# Interview questions (topic-wise)

Use this as a checklist. Try to answer without looking.

## OSI/TCP-IP

- Explain OSI vs TCP/IP. Give examples per layer.
- What is encapsulation?

## L2 (Ethernet/ARP/VLAN)

- MAC vs IP: what changes at each hop?
- What is ARP and when is it used?
- What is a broadcast domain? How do VLANs help?

## L3 (IP/Subnetting/ICMP/NAT)

- Subnet this: `192.168.10.77/26`.
- What is longest prefix match?
- What does NAT do? SNAT vs DNAT?
- What is ICMP used for?

## L4 (TCP/UDP)

- TCP vs UDP: use-cases.
- Why 3-way handshake?
- TIME-WAIT and CLOSE-WAIT meaning?
- Flow control vs congestion control?

## DNS/HTTP/TLS

- DNS resolution and caching.
- HTTP/1.1 vs HTTP/2 vs HTTP/3.
- TLS: what is authenticated? why do cert errors happen?
